Why start with evaluation?

When time is limited, establishing a small, realistic evaluation harness keeps you from optimizing the wrong thing. Build the harness first, then iterate on models against it.

A tiny checklist

  • Write down the task, constraints, and success definition in 3 sentences.
  • Create or reuse 10–20 seed cases that are annoying but representative.
  • Decide one primary metric and one qualitative note to collect per run.
  • Automate logging and charting before adding model complexity.
